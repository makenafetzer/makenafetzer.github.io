Galactic/Extragalactic ULDB Spectroscopic Terahertz Observatory (GUSTO) Mission

A selected NASA Explorer's Program mission, GUSTO seeks to understand the lifecycle of interstellar gas and star-forming clouds in the Milky Way galaxy. As an Ultralong Duration Balloon (ULDB) mission, GUSTO will fly a telescope in the stratosphere over Antarctica measuring emissions from gas within our galaxy and the nearby Large Magellanic Cloud. I worked with principal investigator Dr. Chris Walker and his team to prepare for GUSTO's 2021 launch. I designed and built a PID circuit for NASA’s GUSTO High Altitude Balloon Mission to enable a successful TVAC testing campaign for the Teensy Kinetis Mk66 Microcontroller.
