Mission Operations Intern

Galactic/Extragalactic ULDB Spectroscopic Terahertz Observatory (GUSTO) Mission

A selected NASA Explorer's Program mission, GUSTO seeks to understand the lifecycle of interstellar gas and star-forming clouds in the Milky Way galaxy. As an Ultralong Duration Balloon (ULDB) mission, GUSTO will fly a telescope in the stratosphere over Antarctica measuring emissions from gas within our galaxy and the nearby Large Magellanic Cloud. I'm currently working with principal investigator Chris Walker and his team to prepare for GUSTO's 2021 launch. Tasks ranging from electronics prototyping and testing to project management duties.
